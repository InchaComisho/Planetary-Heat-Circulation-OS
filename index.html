Planetary Heat and Circulation OS
惑星熱・循環OS

A thermal-circulation framework for understanding global warming as a combined problem of accumulated heat, ocean heat content, weakened circulation, degraded carbon sinks, disrupted water cycles, and declining ecosystem-based cooling functions.

惑星熱・循環OSは、地球温暖化を「CO2排出」だけでなく、蓄積熱、海洋熱含量、循環弱化、炭素固定源劣化、水循環の乱れ、生態系冷却機能の低下として理解するための熱循環フレームワークです。

Core Concept

Planetary Heat and Circulation OS treats global warming as a whole-system stability problem. It does not deny the importance of greenhouse gas reduction. Instead, it argues that emissions reduction should be combined with heat-stock awareness, ocean heat monitoring, water-cycle recovery, carbon sink restoration, soil and microbial regeneration, ecosystem recovery, and nature-compatible cooling support.

惑星熱・循環OSは、地球温暖化を地球システム全体の安定性の問題として扱います。温室効果ガス削減を否定するものではなく、排出削減に加えて、熱ストックの把握、海洋熱の監視、水循環回復、炭素固定源再生、土壌・微生物循環、生態系回復、自然補完型冷却を組み合わせる必要があると考えます。
